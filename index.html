About Me

Research Focus

I'm a dedicated 4th year Biomedical Engineering student at Jashore University of Science and Technology (JUST) with hands-on experience in biosensor device development and machine learning applications in healthcare.

My research focuses on developing low-cost, accessible diagnostic technologies for resource-limited settings, particularly in wearable biosensors and lab-on-chip platforms. I combine electrochemical sensing with machine learning algorithms to create intelligent healthcare monitoring systems.
